Our Palm Tree Varieties

Explore the premium palm tree types we install to create stunning tropical landscapes.

Queen Palm

Elegant and fast-growing, Queen Palms add a graceful, feathery look to any landscape, perfect for creating a tropical ambiance.

Robellini Palm

Also known as Pygmy Date Palms, Robellinis are compact and versatile, ideal for smaller spaces or as accent plants.

Sylvester Palm

Known for their striking silver-green fronds, Sylvester Palms are durable and add a bold, majestic presence to any property.

Foxtail Palm

With lush, bushy fronds resembling a fox's tail, Foxtail Palms bring a vibrant, tropical aesthetic to any landscape.

Bismarck Palm

Featuring dramatic, silver-blue fan-like fronds, Bismarck Palms make a stunning statement in large landscapes.

Canary Island Date Palm

With its robust trunk and vibrant green fronds, the Canary Island Date Palm creates a dramatic focal point for large landscapes.
